Psychodrama Session

About Psychodrama

Psychodrama is a powerful therapeutic approach that uses guided dramatic action to examine problems or issues raised by an individual or a group. Through role-playing and dramatic self-presentation, participants gain new insights and perspectives.

Session Structure

  • Warm-up (20 minutes)

    Group exercises to build trust and spontaneity

  • Action (50 minutes)

    Main psychodrama enactment and role-play

  • Sharing (20 minutes)

    Group discussion and personal reflections

Therapeutic Benefits

  • Enhanced Self-awareness
  • Emotional Release
  • Improved Social Skills
  • Problem-solving Skills
  • Personal Growth
